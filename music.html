Music and Sound

The current musical projects I am a part of are:

whythough? - IDM and EDM personal project

svnteen - Hard Trap collaboration project

SWEATY GANG - Hip-Hop/Rap collaboration project (music producer only)

You can find me on splice.

You can stream my debut album on Spotify, or any other music platform you'd like. I am currently collecting remixes from my music producer friends for a remix album due mid-February 2018. If you are interested in getting ahold of any stems, please don't hesitate to shoot me an email at whythough000@gmail.com

I am currently working on my newest audio project, Plantasia, an audio environment that will serve as a platform to interface audio for creating large, human-made datasets for proxies of population genetics data. I have also began to conceptualize the creation of a portable wave field synthesis array.

I am currently finishing a double album with SWEATY GANG, three techno EPs, a svnteen EP, a mandible collaborative EP, a tech house and house EP, an ambisonic compositional field recordings album, an old school gabber collaborative EP, a Flying Lotus/Kaytranada/Thundercat inspired live guitar EP, a worship/gospel single (maybe with a B side), and an ambisonic musicking sound documentary behind the scenes of my sound installation.

Finally, my newest album in the works, "17 weeks", I plan to finish and have released this May 2018. I take most of my inspiration for this album from Stimming, Jacob Collier, and Nina Kraviz.
