Barely Enough Ingredients

August 29, 2017 by Ian

In development, no version avaliable at this time

Website dedicated to storing a clients ingredients in a database, and searching for suitable recipes that fit the clients needs. In addition, it searches nearby grocery stores that are within the clients budget in the event they need more ingredients for a particular recipe.

Uses IBM's Bluemix to make an educated guess on what the user might be interested in eating based on their recent recipes and ratings of each recipe.
