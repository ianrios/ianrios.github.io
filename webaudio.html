Ambisonic and Wavefield Synthesis

January 18, 2018 by Ian

I began work conceptualizing a semester long multichannel project. Currently the plan is to create ambisonic recordings and learn the basics of multichannel music and sound production. I plan to then incorporate this new material in some sort of interactive sound installation that lets users modify and play with sounds and virtual space they hear in the room.

You can follow along with my personal indeveaur of creating a portable wavefield synthesis array here!
