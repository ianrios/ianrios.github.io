Starting up

February 6, 2017 by Ian

last edited February 8, 2017 by Ian

The plan is to have 8 people control over their own frequency, all 8 of which make up a chord. Every time one note is pressed it is louder than the rest and the more times it is pressed, it drowns out the rest. the point is to have all the notes be the same loudness so being able to time correctly is important. Over time, the notes played by each person change, so a song evolves. Whoever is highest in the queue has their note play the loudest.
